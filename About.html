Discover the Magic of Bagan

--------------------------------------------

Bagan is home to over 2,000 ancient pagodas and temples scattered across lush plains and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. From the sunrise views over the pagodas to a serene hot-air balloon ride, every moment in Bagan feels like stepping back in time.

Let us guide you through:

  • The mesmerizing Temples of Bagan, each with its unique story and architecture.
  • Vibrant local markets where you can experience traditional crafts and Myanmar’s renowned hospitality.
  • Hidden villages and cultural spots that showcase the authentic life of the Bagan people.
